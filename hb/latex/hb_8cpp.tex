To establish hydrogen bond between two conformers, the following criteria must be met\-:
\begin{DoxyEnumerate}
\item one conformer is h donor, the other is acceptor.
\item the distance between one donor atom (H) and one acceptor atom (O, N) is between D\-N\-E\-A\-R and D\-F\-A\-R.
\item the angle of h bond should be no less than 90.
